Smt. S.R. Mehta & Sir K. P. Cardiac Institute

Total heart solutions... With complete human care...

Smt. S.R. Mehta & Sir K. P. Cardiac Institute is committed to provide a world class, safe, quality healthcare in cardiology, constantly at affordable rates to members of all strata of society. The institute was started in May 1999 under the Chairmanship of Mr. M. C. Shah, a well -known city industrialist with the aim to deliver cardiac services focused on quality, safety and economy. Now, we are an established super-specialized, state-of-the-art, cardiac hospital. Situated centrally at Sion, our hospital was one of the first to be dedicated solely to the treatment of cardiac cases. Along with the significant number of Angiographies, Angioplasties & By-Pass Surgeries, certain rare procedures like Implantation of Automatic Cardioverter Defibrillator (ACID), Electro physiological study etc. have also been successfully carried out at our hospital.


Dedicated & committed team of super-specialists, Hon. cardiologists & cardio-thoracic surgeons & Orthopaedic have been offering their expert care round the clock in association with equally dedicated professionally updated medical, paramedical & nursing staff, in diagnostics as well as in therapeutic care. Keeping in mind the importance & special needs, the institute also offers preventive programmes in cardiology & special executive as well as comprehensive health check-up programmes.


Headed by well qualified and experienced team of technicians & para-medics, with dedication and focus on quality assurance in all the tests performed at the laboratory. Likewise, the blood bank, a member of the Federation of Bombay Blood Banks, is rated amongst the best in the city; strict vigilance in quality assurance and universal safety precautions in collection, testing, storing and issuing the blood and components; keeping in view the scientific, humanitarian and statutory requirements.
